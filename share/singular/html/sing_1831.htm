D.12.2.6 maximum

Procedure from library atkins.lib (see atkins_lib).

Usage:
maximum(list L);

Return:
the maximal number contained in list L

Example:
 
LIB "atkins.lib";
ring r = 0,x,dp;
list L=465,867,1233,4567,776544,233445,2334,556;
maximum(L);
==> 776544
