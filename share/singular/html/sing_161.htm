4.18.1 qring

SINGULAR offers the opportunity to calculate in quotient rings (factor rings), i.e., rings modulo an ideal. The ideal has to be given as a standard basis. For a detailed description of the concept of rings and quotient rings see Rings and orderings. Beside the construction, an object describing a quotient ring is of type ring.

See qring declaration.
