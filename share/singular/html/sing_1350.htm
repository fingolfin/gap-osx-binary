D.5.2.1 BINresol

Procedure from library resbinomial.lib (see resbinomial_lib).

Usage:
BINresol(J); J ideal

Return:
E-resolution of singularities of a binomial ideal J in terms of the affine charts, see example

Example:
 
LIB "resbinomial.lib";
ring r = 0,(x(1..2)),dp;
ideal J=x(1)^2-x(2)^3;
list B=BINresol(J);
B[1]; // list of final charts
