3.3.4 Coefficient rings

SINGULAR supports coefficient ranges which are not fields, i.e. the integers and the finite rings for a number n. These coefficient rings were implemented in SINGULAR 3.0.5 and at the moment only limited functionality is available.

p-adic numbers

The p-adic integers are the projective limit of the finite rings for n to infinity. Therefore, computations in this ring can be approximated by computations in for large n.
