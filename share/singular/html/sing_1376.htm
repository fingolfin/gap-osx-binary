D.5.2.27 sumlist

Procedure from library resbinomial.lib (see resbinomial_lib).

Usage:
sumlist(L1,L2); L1,L2 lists, (size(L1)==size(L2))

Return:
a list, sum of L1 and L2

Example:
 
LIB "resbinomial.lib";
list L1=1,2,3;
list L2=5,9,7;
sumlist(L1,L2);
==> [1]:
==>    6
==> [2]:
==>    11
==> [3]:
==>    10
