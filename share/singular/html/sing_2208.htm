D.15.14.43 multiDegTensor

Procedure from library multigrading.lib (see multigrading_lib).

Usage:
multiDegTensor(m, n), m,n modules or matrices.

Purpose:
Computes the multigraded tensor product of to multigraded modules.

Return:
A module.

Example:
