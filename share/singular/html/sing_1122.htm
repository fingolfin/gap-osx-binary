D.4.3 assprimeszerodim_lib

Library:
assprimeszerodim.lib
Purpose:
associated primes of a zero-dimensional ideal

Authors:
N. Idrees nazeranjawwad@gmail.com
G. Pfister pfister@mathematik.uni-kl.de

Overview:
A library for computing the associated primes and the radical of a zero-dimensional ideal in the polynomial ring over the rational numbers, Q[x_1,...,x_n], using modular computations.

Procedures:

D.4.3.1 zeroRadical  computes the radical of I
D.4.3.2 assPrimes  computes the associated primes of I
See also: primdec_lib.
