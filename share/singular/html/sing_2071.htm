D.15.2.43 arrBoundedChambers

Procedure from library arr.lib (see arr_lib).

Usage:
arrBoundedChambers(A); arr A

Return:
[int] The number of bounded chambers of an arrangement, which is equal to the evaluation of the Poincare polynomial at -1.

Example:
 
LIB "arr.lib";
ring R = 0,(x,y),dp;
arr A = ideal(x,y,x+y-1);
arrBoundedChambers(A);
==> 1
See also: arrBoundedChambers; arrChambers; arrPoincare.
