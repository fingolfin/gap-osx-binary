D.15.2.39 moebius

Procedure from library arr.lib (see arr_lib).

Usage:
moebius(arrposet P)

Return:
[arrposet] fills in the moebius values of the flats in the poset

Example:
 
LIB "arr.lib";
ring R = 0,(x,y,z,t),dp;
arr A = arrBraid(4);
arrposet P = arrLattice(A);
