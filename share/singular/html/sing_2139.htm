D.15.9.4 gkzFan

Procedure from library gitfan.lib (see gitfan_lib).

Usage:
gkzFan(Q); a: ideal, Q:bigintmat

Purpose:
Returns the GKZ-fan of the matrix Q.

Return:
a fan.

Example:
 
LIB "gitfan.lib";
intmat Q[3][4] =
1,0,1,0,
0,1,0,1,
0,0,1,1;
gkzFan(Q);
