4.24.3.3 vertices

Syntax:
vertices( polytope p )
Type:
intmat
Purpose:
vertices of p
Example:
 
LIB"gfanlib.so";
intmat M[4][3]=
1,0,0,
1,2,0,
1,0,2,
1,2,2,
1,1,1;
polytope p=polytopeViaPoints(M);
vertices(p);
==> 1,2,0,
==> 1,0,0,
==> 1,0,2,
==> 1,2,2
