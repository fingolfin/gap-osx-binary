3.8.5 LIB commands

LIB commands are part of the header of a library.

Syntax:
LIB "lib_1.lib";
...
LIB "lib_r.lib";
Purpose:
Loads libraries used by the library under consideration.
Example:
 
    LIB "primdec.lib";
    LIB "normal.lib";
Note:
The keyword LIB must be followed by at least one space.
