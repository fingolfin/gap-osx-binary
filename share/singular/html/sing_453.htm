5.3.11 voice

Type:
int
Purpose:
shows the nesting level of procedures.
Note:
See Libraries, for a small example how this is used for the display of comments while procedures are executed.
Example:
 
  voice;
==> 1
proc p
{
  voice;
};
p();
==> 2
See dbprint; listvar; printlevel.
