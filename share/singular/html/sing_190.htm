4.22.3.4 coneLink

Syntax:
coneLink( cone c )
Type:
cone
Purpose:
local version of the given cone around the given point of the cone
Example:
 
LIB"gfanlib.so";
intmat M[3][3]=
1,0,0,
0,1,0,
0,0,1;
cone c=coneViaPoints(M);
intvec v=1,0,0;
cone cc=coneLink(c,v);
cc;
