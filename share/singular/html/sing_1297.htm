D.4.21 primdecint_lib

Library:
primdecint.lib
Purpose:
primary decomposition of an ideal in the polynomial ring over the integers

Authors:
G. Pfister pfister@mathematik.uni-kl.de
A. Sadiq afshanatiq@gmail.com
S. Steidel steidel@mathematik.uni-kl.de

Overview:
A library for computing the primary decomposition of an ideal in the polynomial ring over the integers, Z[x_1,...,x_n].
The first procedure 'primdecZ' can be used in parallel.

Reference: Pfister, Sadiq, Steidel , "An Algorithm for primary decomposition in polynomial rings over the integers" , arXiv:1008.2074

Procedures:

D.4.21.1 primdecZ  compute the primary decomposition of ideal I
D.4.21.2 primdecZM  compute the primary decomposition of module I
D.4.21.3 minAssZ  compute the minimal associated primes of I
D.4.21.4 radicalZ  compute the radical of I
D.4.21.5 heightZ  compute the height of I
D.4.21.6 equidimZ  compute the equidimensional part of I
D.4.21.7 intersectZ  compute the intersection of I and J
