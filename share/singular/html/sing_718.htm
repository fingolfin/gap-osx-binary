7.7.11.0. makeUso11
Procedure from library ncalg.lib (see ncalg_lib).

Usage:
makeUso11([p]); p an optional integer (field characteristic)

Return:
a ring, describing U(so_{11})

Note:
You have to activate this ring with the 'setring' command. The presentation of U(so_{11}) is derived from the Chevalley representation of so_{11}, positive resp. negative roots are denoted by x(i) resp. y(i); Cartan elements are denoted by h(i).

Example:
 
LIB "ncalg.lib";
def ncAlgebra = makeUso11();
ncAlgebra;
