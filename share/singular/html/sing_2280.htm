D.16.1.5 groebnerFan

Syntax:
groebnerFan( poly g )
groebnerFan( ideal I )
Assume:
I homogeneous, ground field is the field of rational numbers
Type:
fan
Purpose:
the Groebner fan of g or the Groebner fan I
Note:
set printlevel > 0 for status updates on the computation
Example:
 
LIB "gfanlib.so";
ring r = 0,(x,y),dp;
poly g = x+y+1;
fan f = groebnerFan(g);
f;
