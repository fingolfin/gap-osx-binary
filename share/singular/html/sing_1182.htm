D.4.13.3 gcdMon

Procedure from library monomialideal.lib (see monomialideal_lib).

Usage:
gcdMon (f,g); f,g polynomials.

Return:
a monomial, the greatest common divisor of f and g.

Assume:
f and g are monomials of the basering.

Example:
 
LIB "monomialideal.lib";
ring R = 0,(x,y,z,t),dp;
poly f = x^3*z^5*t^2;
poly g = y^6*z^3*t^3;
gcdMon(f,g);
==> z3t2
