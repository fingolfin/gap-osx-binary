7.2.6.3 resolution related functions (plural)

betti
Betti numbers of a resolution (see betti (plural))
minres
minimizes a free resolution (see minres (plural))
mres
computes a minimal free resolution of an ideal resp. module and a minimal set of generators of the given ideal resp. module (see mres (plural))
nres
computes a free resolution of an ideal resp. module M which is minimized from the second module on (see nres (plural))
