4.18.4 ring operations

Note: Concerning the ground fields and take the following guide lines into consideration:

  • Neither nor may be or .

Example:
 
  ring R1=0,(x,y),dp;
  ring R2=32003,(a,b),dp;
  def R=R1+R2;
  R;
