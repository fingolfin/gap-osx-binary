7.7.2.0. scalarProd
Procedure from library bfun.lib (see bfun_lib).

Usage:
scalarProd(v,w); v,w intvecs

Return:
int, the standard scalar product of v and w

Purpose:
computes the scalar product of two intvecs

Assume:
the arguments are of the same size

Example:
 
LIB "bfun.lib";
intvec v = 1,2,3;
intvec w = 4,5,6;
scalarProd(v,w);
==> 32
