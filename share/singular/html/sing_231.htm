4.23.1.9 isPure

Syntax:
isPure( fan f)
Type:
int
Purpose:
1 iff the fan is pure; 0 otherwise
Example:
 
LIB"gfanlib.so";
fan f=fullFan(2);
isPure(f);
==> 1
fan g=emptyFan(2);
intmat M1[2][2]=
1,0,
0,1;
cone c1=coneViaPoints(M1);
insertCone(g,c1);
isPure(g);
==> 1
intmat M2[1][2]=
0,-1;
cone c2=coneViaPoints(M2);
insertCone(g,c2);
isPure(g,c2);
==> 0
