D.5.3 resgraph_lib

Library:
resgraph.lib
Purpose:
Visualization of Resolution Data

Author:
A. Fruehbis-Krueger, anne@mathematik.uni-kl.de,

Note:
This library uses the external programs surf, graphviz and imagemagick.
Input data is assumed to originate from resolve.lib and reszeta.lib

Procedures:

D.5.3.1 InterDiv  dual graph of resolution of a surface (uses graphviz,imagemagick)
D.5.3.2 ResTree  tree of charts of resolution (uses graphviz,imagemagick)
D.5.3.3 finalCharts  pictures of final charts of surface (uses surf)
