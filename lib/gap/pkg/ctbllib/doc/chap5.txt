  with  groups  that  have  a factor group of type [22XS_3[122X. SectionÂ [14X5.3[114X deals with
  upward  extensions  of a group by a Klein four group. SectionÂ [14X5.4[114X deals with
  downward  extensions of a group by a Klein four group. SectionÂ [14X5.6[114X describes
  the  construction  of  certain Brauer tables. SectionÂ [14X5.7[114X deals with special

  [33X[0;0YExamples can be found in [Breb] and [Bree].[133X
